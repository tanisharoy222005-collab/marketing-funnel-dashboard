# KPI Definitions

## Sessions

Total visits recorded during the reporting period.

---

## Users

Unique visitors interacting with the website.

---

## Engagement Rate

Engaged Sessions ÷ Total Sessions

---

## Conversion Rate

Leads ÷ Sessions

---

## Cost Per Lead (CPL)

Advertising Spend ÷ Leads

---

## Return On Ad Spend (ROAS)

Revenue ÷ Advertising Spend
