# Looker Studio Build Guide

## Overview

This dashboard is designed to provide marketing stakeholders with visibility into acquisition performance, funnel progression, lead generation, and campaign ROI.

Data Sources:

* Google Analytics 4
* Google Ads
* CRM Leads
* BigQuery

---

# Page 1: Executive Overview

## KPI Scorecards

Display:

* Users
* Sessions
* Leads
* Conversion Rate
* Cost Per Lead (CPL)
* Return On Ad Spend (ROAS)

### Charts

1. Sessions Trend (Time Series)
2. Leads Trend (Time Series)
3. Traffic by Channel (Pie Chart)
