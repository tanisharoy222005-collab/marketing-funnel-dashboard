# Executive Summary

The dashboard analysis revealed strong acquisition performance from organic search and paid search channels.

Organic traffic generated the highest volume of sessions, while paid search campaigns delivered the strongest conversion rates.

Key findings:

* Funnel drop-off was highest between CTA Click and Form Start.
* Several landing pages generated traffic but produced low conversion rates.
* Paid campaigns showed varying levels of efficiency across channels.

Recommendations:

1. Simplify lead generation forms.
2. Improve CTA visibility on high-traffic pages.
3. Increase investment in high-performing campaigns.
4. Optimize low-converting landing pages.

Expected Benefits:

* Increased lead generation
* Improved conversion rate
* Lower acquisition costs
* Better marketing ROI
