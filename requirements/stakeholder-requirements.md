# Stakeholder Requirements

## Project Sponsor

Marketing Director

---

## Business Need

The marketing team requires a centralized reporting solution to understand how acquisition channels, campaigns, and landing pages contribute to lead generation and revenue outcomes.

---

## Current Challenges

* Multiple reporting sources
* Limited visibility into funnel performance
* Inconsistent KPI definitions
* Difficult campaign optimization

---

## Success Criteria

The dashboard should provide:

* Daily performance visibility
* Funnel conversion reporting
* Campaign ROI reporting
* Lead quality reporting
* Executive KPI summaries

---

## Stakeholders

### Marketing Director

Needs:

* ROI visibility
* Budget optimization insights

### Performance Marketing Team

Needs:

* Campaign reporting
* CPL monitoring

### Sales Team

Needs:

* Lead quality visibility

### Executive Leadership

Needs:

* High-level KPI reporting

---

## Deliverables

* Marketing Funnel Dashboard
* KPI Governance Framework
* Attribution Documentation
* Data Quality Validation Checks
* Executive Reporting
